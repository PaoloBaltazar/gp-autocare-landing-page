A maintenance detail takes 2–3 hours. Paint correction and coating packages are a full-day or two-day job, since the coating needs time to cure before the car goes back out.

Correction and coating work happens in the controlled lighting of our bay. For maintenance details we offer mobile service within the metro area — just pick the option when you book.
