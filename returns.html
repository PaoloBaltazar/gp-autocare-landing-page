Returns

Not quite right? Send it back.

We stand behind everything we sell. If a product isn't working out, you have 30 days to return it — no fine-print games, no restocking fees.

How returns work

Three steps to a refund

Start the return within 30 days of delivery. Once your item is back with us and checked over, your refund is on its way.

01

Start the return

Email us your order number and what you'd like to do. We'll reply with a prepaid label within one business day.

02

Pack it up

Box the item with the label attached and drop it at any carrier point. Reuse our packaging if you still have it.

03

Get refunded

Once it arrives, we refund your original payment method within 3–5 business days and email you the confirmation.

What we accept

  • Unopened products in their original packaging.
  • Kits with all pieces included, returned as a set.
  • Anything that arrived damaged, leaking or faulty — covered in full, including return shipping.
  • A wrong item we shipped by mistake — that one's on us.

What we can't take back

  • Bottles that have been opened or partly used, for safety reasons.
  • Returns started more than 30 days after delivery.
  • Items without proof of purchase or an order number.
  • Detailing services already performed — those are quoted and confirmed before we start.

Changed your mind on an opened bottle? Reach out anyway — if there's a fault or it didn't perform, we'll make it right. Start a return by emailing hello@gpautocare.com with your order number.
